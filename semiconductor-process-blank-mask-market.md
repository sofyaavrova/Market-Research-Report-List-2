<p><p>A semiconductor process blank mask, also known as a photomask, is an essential tool used in semiconductor manufacturing. It is a transparent plate made of glass or quartz with a patterned layer of chrome or chromium oxide. The pattern on the mask is transferred onto a semiconductor wafer during the lithography process, where the mask acts as a stencil for the deposition or removal of specific materials.</p><p>The Semiconductor Process Blank Mask Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for semiconductors across various industries. The market growth is driven by several factors, including the rising adoption of advanced technologies such as IoT, artificial intelligence, and 5G.
