<p><strong>The Life Preserver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lifebelts</li><li>Horseshoe Lifebuoys</li><li>Survival Floats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The life preserver market comprises three main types: lifebelts, horseshoe lifebuoys, and survival floats. Lifebelts are circular devices that can be worn around a person's waist or chest, providing buoyancy in water. Horseshoe lifebuoys are also circular but come with a flat base, allowing individuals to rest their arms on them for support. Survival floats, on the other hand, are larger devices that can accommodate multiple people and usually include a canopy or roof for protection. These various types cater to different preferences and safety needs in the market.</p></p>
