<p><p>The Chemical Protective Gloves market can be classified into two types: Disposable Gloves and Reusable Gloves. Disposable Gloves are single-use gloves made from materials such as nitrile, latex, or vinyl. They provide protection against chemicals, contaminants, and infectious substances but need to be discarded after use. Reusable Gloves, on the other hand, are designed to be used multiple times. They are made from durable materials like neoprene, rubber, or butyl, and can be decontaminated and reused. Both types cater to the need for hand protection in situations where exposure to chemicals is a concern.</p></p>
