<p><p>A Hosted Private Branch Exchange (PBX) is a cloud-based telephony system that allows businesses to manage their telephone communications without the need for an on-premises PBX system. With a hosted PBX, all the necessary equipment and infrastructure are hosted and maintained by a third-party service provider, while businesses can access and control their communications through an internet connection.</p><p>The hosted PBX market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to various factors. Firstly, the rising adoption of cloud-based solutions by businesses is driving the demand for hosted PBX systems. This is primarily because hosted PBX offers cost-effectiveness, scalability, and flexibility compared to traditional on-premises PBX solutions. Additionally, the increasing trend of remote working and virtual offices is further propelling the market growth as hosted PBX allows employees to connect and communicate seamlessly regardless of their location.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing several technological advancements and innovations to enhance the functionality and features of hosted PBX systems.
