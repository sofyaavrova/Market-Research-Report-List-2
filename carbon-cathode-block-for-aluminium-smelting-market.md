There are various types of carbon cathode blocks available in the market, including graphitic cathodes, graphitized cathodes, and others. </p><p>Graphitic cathodes are made from high-quality carbon materials that have been graphitized, resulting in improved electrical conductivity and resistance to oxidation. Graphitized cathodes, on the other hand, undergo a further graphitization process, enhancing their physical and chemical properties even more. </p><p>The "others" category encompasses cathode blocks made from different carbon materials or variations of the above two types. These various types cater to different smelting requirements and offer a range of options to aluminium producers.</p></p>
