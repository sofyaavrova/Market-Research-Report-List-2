<p><p>Large screen ebook readers are available in two main types - E-paper and LCD. E-paper displays mimic the appearance of traditional ink on paper and provide a glare-free reading experience even in direct sunlight. They offer improved battery life and are easier on the eyes. On the other hand, LCD (liquid crystal display) screens provide vibrant colors and are suitable for multimedia content. They offer a backlit display, which makes them readable in the dark. However, LCD screens may cause eye strain after prolonged use.</p></p>
