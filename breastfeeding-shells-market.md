<p><p>Breastfeeding shells are devices that can be used to address various nursing issues faced by breastfeeding mothers. There are two main types available in the market: silicone breastfeeding shells and polypropylene breastfeeding shells. Silicone shells are soft, flexible, and more comfortable to wear. They are designed to collect breast milk leaks and protect sore nipples by creating a barrier between the breast and clothing. On the other hand, polypropylene shells are more rigid and provide better airflow to promote healing of cracked nipples. Both types serve to support breastfeeding mothers and enhance their nursing experience.</p></p>
