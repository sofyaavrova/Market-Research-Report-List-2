<p><p>The plastic recycling additive market can be divided into three types: plasticizers, stabilizers, and others. Plasticizers are additives that are used to enhance the softness and flexibility of recycled plastics, making them easier to process and mold. Stabilizers, on the other hand, help to prevent degradation and maintain the mechanical properties of the recycled plastics during processing and use. The "others" category includes a wide range of additives, such as colorants, antioxidants, and flame retardants, which are used to modify specific properties or meet specific requirements of the recycled plastics.</p></p>
