<p><p>Laptop chargers can be classified into two main market types: AC and DC chargers. AC chargers are designed to convert the alternating current from a wall outlet into the direct current required by laptops. These chargers have a plug that can be attached to standard electrical outlets. On the other hand, DC chargers are used for charging laptops directly from a car battery or any other DC power source. These chargers have a cigarette lighter plug or a connector that can be used with a DC power source.</p></p>
