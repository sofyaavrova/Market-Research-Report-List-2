<p><p>The Distance Measurement System (DMS) market encompasses various types of technologies used for measuring distance. Optical measurement systems utilize the principles of reflection and refraction of light to calculate distances. Laser measurement systems utilize laser beams to determine distances by measuring the time it takes for the laser light to bounce back from a target. Other measurement systems may include ultrasonic, infrared, or radar-based techniques. These systems find applications in industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, and robotics, providing accurate and reliable distance measurements for various purposes.</p></p>
