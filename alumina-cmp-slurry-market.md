<p><p>The Alumina CMP Slurry market is primarily categorized into two types: Nano Grade and Micron Grade. Nano Grade slurry consists of alumina particles that are extremely small, typically less than 100 nanometers in size. This grade is ideal for applications that require high precision and fine polishing, such as semiconductor manufacturing. On the other hand, Micron Grade slurry contains larger alumina particles, typically ranging from 1 to 5 micrometers in size. This grade is suitable for general-purpose polishing and is commonly used in glass and metal industries. Both grades cater to specific requirements and offer unique benefits in various industries.</p></p>
