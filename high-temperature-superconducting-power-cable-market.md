<p><p>High temperature superconducting power cables are categorized into three types: YBCO cables, Bi-2212 cables, and Bi2223 cables. YBCO (yttrium barium copper oxide) cables are made from a compound of yttrium, barium, copper, and oxygen. Bi-2212 cables are composed of a compound of bismuth, strontium, calcium, copper, and oxygen. Bi2223 cables consist of a compound of bismuth, strontium, calcium, copper, and oxygen as well, but with an additional layer of a compound containing thallium. These cables are used to transmit high amounts of electrical power with minimal loss, as they operate at extremely low temperatures.</p></p>
