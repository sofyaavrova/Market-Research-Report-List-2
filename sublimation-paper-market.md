<p><p>The sublimation paper market is divided into three main types: laser printing, inkjet printing, and dye-sublimation printing. Laser printing uses heat to fuse toner onto the paper, resulting in high-quality and precise prints. Inkjet printing, on the other hand, utilizes droplets of ink sprayed onto the paper, providing vibrant and detailed images. Lastly, dye-sublimation printing transfers dye onto the paper using heat, creating long-lasting and fade-resistant prints. These different market types cater to various printing needs, offering versatility and specific advantages in the sublimation paper industry.</p></p>
