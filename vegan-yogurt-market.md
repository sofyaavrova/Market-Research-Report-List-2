<p><p>Vegan yogurt is a plant-based alternative to traditional dairy yogurt. Growing health consciousness, rising prevalence of lactose intolerance, and increasing awareness about the cruelty associated with animal farming are driving the demand for plant-based products, including vegan yogurt. With a rising demand for plant-based products, increasing health consciousness, and innovative product offerings, the market is expected to expand rapidly.
