<p><p>Shea Nut Butter is available in two main market types: Raw and Unrefined Shea Nut Butter, and Refined Shea Nut Butter. Raw and Unrefined Shea Nut Butter is made from shea nuts that are cold-pressed to extract the natural butter without any additional processing or additives. It maintains the natural color, scent, and nutrients of shea nuts. On the other hand, Refined Shea Nut Butter undergoes further processing, such as bleaching and deodorizing, to create a smoother and more consistent product. This removes some of the natural color, scent, and nutrients of shea nuts.</p></p>
