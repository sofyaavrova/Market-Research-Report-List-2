<p><strong>The 2 Phase Stepper Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar Type</li><li>Unipolar Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bipolar type of 2 phase stepper motors is a popular market choice due to its higher torque and efficiency. It operates by reversing the current flow through the motor windings, allowing for precise control and accuracy. On the other hand, the unipolar type offers a simpler design and lower cost. It operates by energizing each winding individually, resulting in lower torque but convenient control. Both types have their advantages and applications, catering to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
