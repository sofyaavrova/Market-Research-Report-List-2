<p><p>Electronic earmuffs are a type of hearing protection designed to minimize loud noises in various environments. Passive earmuffs offer basic noise reduction by using acoustic insulation materials to block sound. They are suitable for low to moderate noise levels. Active earmuffs, on the other hand, employ electronic technology to enhance hearing protection. They have built-in microphones that capture external sounds and transmit them through speakers after amplification, allowing users to hear normal sounds while still reducing harmful noise levels. Active earmuffs are ideal for high-noise environments, such as shooting ranges or construction sites.</p></p>
