<p><p>The propylene-petrochemicals market comprises two main types: homopolymer and copolymer. Homopolymer refers to a type of propylene-based polymer that is made from a single type of monomer. It possesses uniform properties and tends to have higher strength and stiffness. Copolymer, on the other hand, is a propylene-based polymer made from two or more different monomers. It offers a wider range of properties and is often used in applications that require flexibility, impact resistance, or transparency. Both homopolymer and copolymer play significant roles in various industries, including packaging, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
