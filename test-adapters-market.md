<p><p>Test adapters are devices used to connect different types of fiber optic connectors for testing purposes. Single-mode test adapters are used for single-mode fibers, which have a narrow core size and are ideal for long-distance transmission. Multimode test adapters are used for multimode fibers, which have a larger core size and are suitable for short-distance transmission. Hybrid test adapters combine both single-mode and multimode connectors to accommodate different fiber optic cables. These adapters allow technicians to easily test and measure the performance of fiber optic networks.</p></p>
