FOSB, or Front Opening Shipping Box, performs a similar role, but is used for packaging semiconductor wafers during transport between different fabs or semiconductor manufacturing facilities. Both FOUP and FOSB play crucial roles in safeguarding the delicate semiconductor wafers and ensuring their proper handling, storage, and transportation.</p></p>
