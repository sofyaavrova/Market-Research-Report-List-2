<p><p>Fluorescent fingerprint powders are classified into two types: mineral pigment-based and plant-based. They are available in various colors like white, green, and yellow. On the other hand, plant-based powders are made from natural ingredients and are an eco-friendly alternative. Both types cater to the specific needs of forensic experts in identifying latent fingerprints.</p></p>
