<p><p>The natural whey protein market is categorized into two types: concentrate form and isolate form. Whey protein concentrate is made by filtering the whey to remove some of the lactose, fat, and minerals, resulting in a powder with around 70-80% protein content. On the other hand, whey protein isolate undergoes further processing to remove almost all the lactose and fat, resulting in a powder with over 90% protein content. Both forms offer varying levels of protein concentration, allowing consumers to choose based on their preferences and dietary requirements.</p></p>
