<p><p>Planimeters are measuring tools used to determine the area of irregularly shaped two-dimensional objects. The market for planimeters can be categorized into various types. Polar planimeters measure the area within a closed curve by tracing its outline using a polar coordinate system. Linear planimeters use a linear axis to calculate the area enclosed by the curve. Prytz planimeters are specialized instruments that incorporate a needle adjustment feature. There are other planimeter variants available in the market, each with its own unique features and functionalities to suit different applications and user preferences.</p></p>
