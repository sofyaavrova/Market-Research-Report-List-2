<p><p>Rack Power Module market types are differentiated based on their height, measured in rack units (U). 1U, 2U, 3U, and 4U refer to specific heights of the modules that fit into racks. For example, a 1U module is approximately 1.75 inches tall, while a 2U is around 3.5 inches. Similarly, a 3U rack power module is about 5.25 inches high, and a 4U module is roughly 7 inches tall. "Others" encompasses any size that doesn't fall within these standard units, providing flexibility for specific requirements or custom designs.</p></p>
