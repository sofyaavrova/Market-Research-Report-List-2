<p><p>The Space Light Modulator (SLM) market includes various types, such as Optically Addressed SLM, Electrically Addressed SLM, and Others. Optically Addressed SLMs use light signals to control the modulation of light, which makes them suitable for applications like holography and optical data storage. On the other hand, Electrically Addressed SLMs utilize electrical signals to modulate light, making them ideal for applications such as displays and optical communication. The "Others" category includes SLM technologies that don't fall under the aforementioned types, such as magnetically addressed SLMs or acousto-optic devices.</p></p>
