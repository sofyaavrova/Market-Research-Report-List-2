<p><p>Exercise bikes are categorized into two main types: upright exercise bikes and recumbent exercise bikes. Upright exercise bikes mimic the traditional design of a regular bicycle, with the rider in an upright position. These bikes provide a more intense workout, engaging the core and upper body. On the other hand, recumbent exercise bikes feature a reclined seat and backrest, providing a more comfortable and supportive workout experience. Recumbent bikes are ideal for individuals with back problems or those seeking a low-impact workout, as they put less stress on the joints.</p></p>
