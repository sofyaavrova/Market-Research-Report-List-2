<p><p>Soy-based milk substitutes are available in two main types: soy milk powder and liquid soy milk. Soy milk powder is a powdered form of soy-based milk that needs to be mixed with water before consumption. It is convenient and has a longer shelf life compared to liquid soy milk. On the other hand, liquid soy milk is already in a ready-to-drink form and does not require any additional preparation. It is commonly found in cartons or bottles and is favored for its freshness and convenience. Both options offer a dairy-free alternative to regular milk for those with lactose intolerance or dietary preferences.</p></p>
