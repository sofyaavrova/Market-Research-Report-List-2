<p><p>The flashing doorbell market consists of two main types: wired and wireless. Wired flashing doorbells are physically connected to the home's electrical system, providing a reliable and constant power source. They typically require professional installation but offer a stable connection and consistent functionality. On the other hand, wireless flashing doorbells operate without any physical wiring and rely on wireless signals to transmit information. They are easier to install, as they simply require battery or rechargeable power sources, but may have occasional connectivity issues due to signal interference.</p></p>
