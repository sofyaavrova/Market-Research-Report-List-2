<p><p>Environmentally friendly plastic bags and sacks are made from various types of materials, including polylactic acid (PLA), polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), starch blends, and others. PHA bags are produced by bacteria and degrade naturally in the environment. Starch blends are made by combining starch with other biodegradable materials, reducing the negative impact on the environment. Other types of environmentally friendly bags may include biopolyethylene, which is derived from bioethanol production.</p></p>
