<p><p>Dedicated Energy Metering IC Chip market includes two main types: Simplex and Polyphase. Simplex meters are commonly used in single-phase electrical systems where only one current and one voltage measurement is required. Polyphase meters, on the other hand, are used in multi-phase electrical systems where multiple current and voltage measurements are needed, making them capable of monitoring and measuring power consumption in three-phase or higher electrical networks. Both types play a vital role in accurately measuring and monitoring energy usage in various applications.</p></p>
