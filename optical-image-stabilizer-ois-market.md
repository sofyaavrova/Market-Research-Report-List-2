<p><p>The Optical Image Stabilizer (OIS) market includes two main types: the Lens Shift Method and the Module Tilt Method. The Lens Shift Method utilizes a small motor to shift the lens elements to counteract camera shake and produce a stable image. On the other hand, the Module Tilt Method employs a tilting mechanism to compensate for camera movement. Both technologies reduce blur and improve image quality by compensating for shaky hands or movement, enabling users to capture clear and sharp photos or videos.</p></p>
