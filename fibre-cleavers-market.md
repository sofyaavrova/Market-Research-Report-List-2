<p><p>Fibre cleavers are tools used in the field of fiber optic communications to precisely and accurately cleave or cut optical fibers. They are primarily utilized in the termination and splicing processes of optical fibers to achieve smooth, clean, and flat end-faces, which are essential for efficient light transmission. Fibre cleavers help in attaining low insertion loss and high return loss, thereby enhancing the overall performance of fiber optic networks.</p><p>The fibre cleavers market is expected to grow at a steady pace during the forecast period.
