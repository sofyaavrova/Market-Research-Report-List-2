<p><p>Ventilated stretch film is a type of packaging material that allows air to circulate through it, providing breathability for products during storage and transportation. There are two main types of ventilated stretch film: perforated manual film and perforated machine film. Perforated manual film is designed for manual application and has pre-drilled holes that allow air to flow through. Perforated machine film, on the other hand, is used with automated stretch wrapping machines and has precise perforations for optimal airflow. Both types offer efficient ventilation and protection for goods while ensuring proper airflow to prevent moisture buildup or spoilage.</p></p>
