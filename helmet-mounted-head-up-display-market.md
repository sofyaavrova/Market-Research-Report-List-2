<p><ul><li>Inside the Helmet</li><li>Outside the Helmet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Helmet-mounted Head Up Display (HUD) market can be categorized into two types: Inside the Helmet and Outside the Helmet. Inside the Helmet HUDs are integrated directly into the helmet, providing critical information like navigation, target identification, and mission data directly in the pilot's line of sight. Outside the Helmet HUDs, on the other hand, project the information onto a transparent visor in front of the pilot's eyes without the need for a helmet, ensuring a wider field of vision. These different types offer varying features and advantages for users in different applications.</p></p>
