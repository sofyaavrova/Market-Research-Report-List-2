<p><ul><li>Alkali Glass</li><li>E-glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid crystal glass substrate market is divided into two main types: Alkali Glass and E-glass. Alkali Glass is a type of glass that contains alkali metal oxides, such as sodium or potassium, in its composition. It is known for its high chemical durability and resistance to heat and moisture, making it suitable for various applications in the liquid crystal display (LCD) industry. On the other hand, E-glass refers to a type of glass that is electrically conductive, primarily used in the construction of electronic devices and telecommunications equipment. It offers good chemical resistance, high tensile strength, and excellent electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
