Lastly, in the Phenol Hydrogenation method, phenol undergoes hydrogenation to yield adipic acid. These different processes offer various advantages and disadvantages, such as cost-effectiveness, efficiency, and environmental impact, which impact the overall market dynamics of high purity adipic acid.</p></p>
