<p><p>The three-phase bridge rectifiers market is categorized into three types: SMD/SMT, Screw Mount, and Through Hole. SMD/SMT refers to surface mount devices or surface mount technology, where components are mounted directly onto PCBs using soldering techniques. Screw Mount involves mounting the rectifiers through screws, providing a secure and robust connection. On the other hand, Through Hole uses traditional component mounting methods, where the rectifiers are inserted into pre-drilled holes on the PCB and soldered in place. These different market types cater to different assembly and installation requirements in various applications.</p></p>
