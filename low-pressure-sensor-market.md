<p><p>The low pressure sensor market can be classified into two types: wired sensor market and wireless sensor market. Wired sensors are connected to the monitoring system with the help of physical wires, ensuring a stable and reliable connection. On the other hand, wireless sensors use wireless technology to transmit data to the monitoring system, eliminating the need for physical wires. This provides flexibility and convenience in installations, especially in remote or hard-to-reach areas. Both types serve the purpose of monitoring low pressure levels, but differ in terms of connectivity and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
