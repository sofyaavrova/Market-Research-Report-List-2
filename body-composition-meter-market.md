<p><ul><li>Digital</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The body composition meter market consists of two main types: digital and electric. Digital body composition meters use digital technology to measure and analyze body composition elements such as body fat percentage, muscle mass, water content, and bone density. They are user-friendly, often featuring sleek designs and easy-to-read displays. Electric body composition meters, on the other hand, use electrical impedance to measure body composition. They typically require the user to step on a device that sends a low electrical current through the body to measure various parameters. Both types serve the purpose of tracking and monitoring an individual's body composition accurately and conveniently.</p></p>
